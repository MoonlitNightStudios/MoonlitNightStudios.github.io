About me

I am Srinath, a video game programmer who specializes in gameplay programming and developing tools for the unreal engine editor. This is my portfolio! But before that, here's a little about myself.

I love action adventure games, RPGs and simple indie games with interesting game designs. Some of my favorite games include:

  • Sekiro: Shadows die twice
  • Armored Core VI: Fires of Rubicon
  • Black Myth Wukong
  • Ori and the Blind Forest & Ori and the Will of the Wisps
  • Tunic
  • The Darksiders franchise
  • Cyberpunk 2077
  • Ghost of Tsushima

There are even more but this is already a long list! Outside of gaming I love cooking and hiking. I love to try new cuisines from all over the world. I used to tinker with robots and I am planning on buying a 3D printer soon so I can get back to building some new ones. So maybe you can expect to see robots here sometime!

I also like learning more about the gaming industry and creating new games and tools in Unreal Engine. My experience in game dev has been primarily using Unreal Engine but I have also worked with Unity and have worked with propritary engines written in C++.

I have also designed a few games as a hobby, prepared references, concepts, put together a Game Design Document and worked on prototyping them.
